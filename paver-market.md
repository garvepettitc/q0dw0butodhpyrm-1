<p><p>The paver market encompasses various types of paving machines, primarily mechanical and hydrostatic pavers. Mechanical pavers utilize traditional transmission systems, relying on mechanical gears for movement, making them cost-effective and straightforward for smaller projects. In contrast, hydrostatic pavers employ hydraulic systems for smoother operation and better control, allowing for precise adjustments and improved performance on larger or complex jobsites. Both types cater to different construction needs, influencing their adoption based on project scale and operational requirements.</p></p>
