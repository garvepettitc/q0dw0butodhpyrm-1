<p><p>The spray dried milk powder market consists primarily of two main types: full cream milk powder and skimmed milk powder. Full cream milk powder retains its fat content, offering a rich flavor and creamy texture, making it popular in food products like chocolates and desserts. Skimmed milk powder, on the other hand, is produced by removing most of the fat, providing a lower-calorie option, widely used in bakery items, beverages, and nutritional supplements, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
