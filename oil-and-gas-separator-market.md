<p><ul><li>Horizontal Separator</li><li>Vertical Separator</li><li>Spherical Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas separator market includes three main types: horizontal separators, vertical separators, and spherical separators. Horizontal separators are designed for efficient separation of oil, gas, and water, commonly used in production facilities. Vertical separators occupy less space and are favored in offshore applications. Spherical separators provide a compact design and are effective in high-pressure conditions. Each type has specific applications and advantages, catering to various needs in the oil and gas extraction and processing sectors.</p></p>
